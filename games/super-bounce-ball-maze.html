About this game

Super Bounce Ball Maze is a fast, reflex-focused maze game. Control a bouncing ball and navigate through tricky levels filled with obstacles. The goal is to reach the end of each maze while avoiding hazards and using bounce pads and walls to your advantage.

How to play
← / → — Move left / right
↑ or Space — Jump (if available)
Use momentum to reach higher platforms and avoid hazards. Timing is key!
Tips
  • Watch the bounce pads — they change your jump height.
  • Sometimes a short hop followed by a wall-bounce gets you further than a full jump.
  • Replay levels to learn safe paths and shortcuts.
